## How we work

**R1 — Nothing produced without a validated brief.**
Before any document is written, intent and structure are agreed. One exchange. Then Claude writes.

**R2 — Manifesto is the constitution.**
Every document answers to MANIFESTE.md. Conflict → Manifesto wins.

**R3 — Complete or placeholder.**
No half-written sections in public files. Either content is there, or a `*[Section not yet written — Issue #N]*` placeholder marks it.

**R4 — One pass, then targeted review.**
Claude writes the full draft. Alex reads and flags specific lines. No word-by-word iteration.

**R5 — Thursday goal: contributor-ready, not content-complete.**
Clean structure + working automation + welcoming entry points = launch success.

**R6 — _project/ is internal and raw.**
These files are operational notes. They do not need to be beautiful.

**R7 — Alex validates before anything is pushed.**
Claude delivers files. Alex confirms "good to push." Claude never assumes authorisation.
