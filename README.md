## What this is

A field guide — not a marketing pitch. Built from three failed attempts and five months of hands-on work configuring OpenClaw for real business use.

Every section is grounded in practice. Every template is ready to copy. The full journey is covered: understanding what OpenClaw is, installing it, configuring it, making it yours, and keeping it running.

A seventh chapter documents local context — legislation, tools, and cultural practices — by geography, starting with France (fr-FR).

---

## Who this is for

Entrepreneurs, freelancers, artisans, developers, and enterprise teams who want OpenClaw to work for their business — not just answer questions. The guide is written in English. French context is documented in [Chapter 7](sections/07-localisation/fr-FR.md).

## How to contribute

**Without GitHub:**  
Read at [www.openclawfieldplaybook.com](https://www.openclawfieldplaybook.com) — share it with someone who needs it.

**Via Issues (no code required):**  
- [Correct something](https://github.com/alexwill87/openclawfieldplaybook/issues/new?template=correction.yml) — typo, broken link, factual error
- [Propose a section](https://github.com/alexwill87/openclawfieldplaybook/issues/new?template=suggestion.yml) — new content, use case, locale

**Via Pull Request:**  
Fork → edit a file in `sections/` → open a PR. An AI agent reviews every PR and posts a first analysis. Full guide: [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md)
