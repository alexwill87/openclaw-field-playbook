# Code of Conduct

This project is a professional, welcoming space for practitioners of all backgrounds.

## Standards

- Contribute based on substance, not status
- Disagree with arguments, not with people
- Label your experience level — "I tested this" vs "I think this"
- French and English contributions receive equal respect
- New contributors are mentored, not dismissed

## Not acceptable

- Personal attacks or dismissive language
- Contributions made in bad faith (spam, self-promotion unrelated to the project)
- Claiming credit for others' work

## Enforcement

Issues are handled by the project founder. Repeated violations result in removal from the contributor list.

Contact: open an Issue labelled `conduct` or reach Alex directly via LinkedIn.
