# 1.8 -- What does it really cost?

> The most important question. Here are the real numbers, no surprises.

---

## Monthly cost

| Item | What it is | Cost |
|-------|-------------|------|
| Server (VPS) | A small remote computer running your agent | 4-8 EUR/month |
| Artificial intelligence | The "brain" of the agent (via OpenRouter) | 5-15 EUR/month |
| Communication channel | Telegram, email... | Free |
| **Total** | | **10-25 EUR/month** |

That's cheaper than a Spotify + Netflix subscription.

---

## Startup cost

You have two options:

### Option 1: You hire an installer

A professional installs and configures everything for you in 3 hours.

| Item | Cost |
|-------|------|
| Installation and configuration | 300-500 EUR (one-time) |
| 1-hour training | Often included |

You don't need any technical skills. It's like calling an electrician to install an electrical panel.

### Option 2: You follow the guide yourself

If you're comfortable with a computer (you don't need to be a developer), the guide is free and comprehensive.

| Item | Cost |
|-------|------|
| The playbook | Free |
| Your time | 6-10 hours |

---

## What it brings you back

The calculation is straightforward. Take a tradesperson who responds to clients 48 hours late:

| Situation | Before | After |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Evening admin time | 2h/day | 15 min/day |
| Response time to prospects | 48 hours | 5 minutes |
| Prospects lost per month | 3-5 | 0-1 |
| Quotes sent per week | 3 | 7-8 |

If one quote in three becomes a job, and an average job brings in 800 EUR, the 2-3 additional quotes per week represents roughly **2,000 EUR of additional revenue per month**.

For an investment of 15-25 EUR/month.

---

## Hidden costs (there aren't any)

- **No commitment.** You can stop whenever you want.
- **No license.** OpenClaw is open source.
- **No update fees.** Updates are included.
- **No "premium" tier.** Everything is in the free version.

The only variable cost is AI usage: the more your agent works, the more it costs. But even a very active agent rarely exceeds 15 EUR/month for a tradesperson or small business.
