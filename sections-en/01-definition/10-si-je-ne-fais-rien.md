# 1.10 -- What happens if I do nothing?

> No one is forcing you to use AI. But here's what it concretely means to change nothing.

---

## Your situation today

You are an entrepreneur, craftsperson, or you manage a small business. Your day:

- 8-10h on your core business (projects, clients, production)
- 2-3h in the evening on admin (emails, quotes, follow-ups, accounting)
- Prospects waiting 24-48h before getting a response
- Unpaid invoices that you follow up on "when you think about it"
- No clear vision of your week without opening 4 different tools

It works. But it runs on your personal energy.

---

## What's changing around you

- Your competitors using AI respond to prospects in 5 minutes. You respond in 48h. The client chooses whoever responds first.
- Platforms (Google, social media) favor businesses that publish regularly. You don't have time to publish.
- Clients expect faster and faster responses. "I'll call you back tomorrow" is no longer enough.

It's not a technology issue. It's a matter of response time and consistency.

---

## The 3 concrete risks

### 1. Losing prospects due to slowness

A prospect contacts 3 craftspeople at the same time. Whoever responds first has a 50% chance of landing the contract. If you respond tomorrow evening, they've already signed with someone else.

### 2. Burning out on admin work

2 hours of admin each evening is 60 hours a month. 60 hours you're not spending with your family, resting, or developing your business. In the long term, it's the path to burnout.

### 3. Stagnating while others accelerate

AI is not a fad. It's a tool that cuts administrative time by 4. Entrepreneurs who adopt it now get ahead. Not because they're better — because they have more time.

---

## What "doing nothing" really costs

Let's calculate over a year:

| Item | Cost of inaction |
|-------|-------------------|
| Prospects lost (3/month x 800 EUR) | ~28,800 EUR/year in lost revenue |
| Admin time (2h/day x 250 days) | 500 hours/year |
| Forgotten follow-ups (2 invoices/month) | ~4,800 EUR of cash flow blocked |

Against an investment of 15-25 EUR/month and 3h of setup.

---

## The good news

You don't need to change everything at once. Most entrepreneurs start with **a single use case**:

- Email triage (most common)
- Draft quotes
- Invoice follow-ups

Just one of these three is enough to see the difference in a week.

---

## In summary

Doing nothing isn't free. It costs time, prospects, and energy. The question isn't "is AI for me?" but "can I afford to keep going as I am?".
