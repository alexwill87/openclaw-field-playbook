# 1.9 -- Is this complicated for me?

> You are not a developer. You know nothing about "Docker" or "SSH". Is this still for you? Yes, and here's why.

---

## Two paths, two difficulty levels

### Path 1: You have someone install it (recommended)

**Difficulty: like having an alarm system installed in your home.**

You call an installer. They handle everything. In 3 hours, your agent is ready. You get a 30-minute training session to learn how to use Telegram with your agent.

What you need to know how to do:
- Use Telegram (as simple as WhatsApp)
- Reply "ok" or "no" to a message
- Dictate a sample quote once so the agent learns your format

That's it.

### Path 2: You install it yourself

**Difficulty: like assembling an IKEA piece of furniture with the instructions.**

The playbook guides you step by step. Every command is written out, every expected result is shown. You don't need to understand what the commands do — you copy-paste them.

What you need to know how to do:
- Type commands in a terminal (the guide shows you how)
- Follow instructions in order
- Read error messages (the guide explains them all)

Plan for 6-10 hours spread over a weekend.

---

## What you do NOT need to know

- How to program (no code to write)
- How AI works (you use it, you don't build it)
- How to manage a server (once installed, it runs on its own)
- How to speak English (the entire guide is in French)

---

## The moments where you might get stuck

Let's be honest: here are the 3 moments where people struggle the most.

| Moment | Why it's hard | The solution |
|--------|---------------|--------------|
| Choosing a server (VPS) | Too many choices, technical jargon | The guide recommends one provider with the right settings |
| Connecting email | Every provider has its own procedure | The guide covers Gmail, Outlook, and business email |
| Writing the agent's instructions | You need to explain your business to the agent | The guide provides templates to fill in |

None of these obstacles are technical. They are choices to make, and the guide helps you make them.

## A simple comparison

| Daily task | Difficulty | Using OpenClaw |
|-----------|------------|-----------------|
| Using WhatsApp | Easy | Easier (you reply "ok") |
| Filing a URSSAF declaration online | Medium | Equivalent (follow a guide) |
| Installing an internet box | Medium | Equivalent (plug in and configure) |
| Creating a website yourself | Difficult | Simpler (no design work) |

---

## The safety net

- **The agent never does anything on its own.** During the first month, everything goes through your approval.
- **You can stop everything with one click.** If you don't like it, you shut down the server. No consequences.
- **You're not alone.** The community answers questions, and an installer can take over at any time.

---

## In summary

If you know how to use WhatsApp and reply to an email, you can use OpenClaw. The real question isn't "is this complicated?", it's "do I want to save 2 hours a day?".
