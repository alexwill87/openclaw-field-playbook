7. Localization

The playbook content is universal. This chapter documents what changes based on local context -- legislation, ecosystem, tools, cultural practices.

Chapters 0 through 6 apply everywhere. This chapter is the only place where content differs by country: legal framework (GDPR, CCPA, PIPEDA...), local vendors, tax and cultural conventions. Check the sheet for your country to adapt your installation, or contribute a new locale if yours is missing.

See sections/00-reading-guide.md for the full language contract of the playbook.

How to add a locale

Open an Issue with the new-locale label specifying: - The locale code (e.g. de-DE) - A summary of what is locally specific - Whether you can write the first draft
